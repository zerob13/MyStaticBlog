The Big Bang Theory Theme(TBBT的主题曲，完整版）

2010-03-15

推荐这首歌曲的原因有两个，第一，最近刚刚看了big bang的第一季第二季以及还没完的第三季～第二，歌词里面的东西很有趣，是那种能够促使我内分泌大量分泌肾上腺素的名词～

所以，这个也是我第一次对歌词加了各种注释。。。

所谓的The Big Bang，原意应该是指物理上面的对于宇宙起源的一种学说，中文称为大爆炸理论～

Our whole universe was in a hot dense(稠密) state, Then nearly fourteen billion years ago expansion started. Wait… The Earth began to cool, The autotrophs(自养生物) began to drool, Neanderthals（尼安德特人，一种灭绝了的人属，与现代人属于不同人种，现代人属于智人） developed tools, We built a wall（长城） (we built the pyramids（金字塔）), Math, science, history, unraveling the mysteries, That all started with the big bang（大爆炸）!

“Since the dawn of man” is really not that long, As every galaxy（星系） was formed in less time than it takes to sing this song. A fraction of a second（几分之一秒） and the elements（指各种元素） were made. The bipeds（两足动物） stood up straight, The dinosaurs all met their fate, They tried to leap（指基因突变，或者说进化） but they were late And they all died (they froze their asses off) The oceans and Pangea See ya wouldn’t wanna be ya Set in motion by the same big bang!

It all started with the big BANG!（一切起源于大爆炸。。。主要是因为主角之一的Sheldon是弦理论的支持者）

It’s expanding ever outward but one day It will cause the stars to go the other way,（这两句指宇宙膨胀说） Collapsing ever inward, we won’t be here, it won’t be hurt Our best and brightest figure that it’ll make an even bigger bang!

Australopithecus（南方古猿，一种强壮脑容量很小的祖先，灭绝了，智人是它的后裔） would really have been sick of us Debating how we’re here, they’re catching deer (we’re catching viruses（指电脑病毒）) Religion or astronomy (Descartes or Deuteronomy) It all started with the big bang!

Music and mythology, Einstein and astrology It all started with the big bang! It all started with the big BANG!
